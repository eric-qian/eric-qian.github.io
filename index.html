About

I am a Senior Research Analyst for the Federal Reserve Bank of New York's Time Series Analysis Team.
I graduated with Highest Distinction from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2018 with majors in Mathematics (B.S.) and Statistics (B.S.). I also received a minor in Economics from Duke University under the Robertson Scholars Program.
All views expressed are my own, and do not reflect those of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York nor Federal Reserve System.
Interests. Macroeconomics, business cycles, monetary economics, macroeconometrics
