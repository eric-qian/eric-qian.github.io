Eric Qian

I am a Senior Research Analyst at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York on the Time Series Analysis Team, working on academic research, policy analysis, and macroeconomic forecasting.
I graduated with Highest Distinction from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2018 with majors in Mathematics (BS) and Statistics and Analytics (BS). Under the Robertson Scholars Program, a dual enrollment full scholarship program, I received a minor in Economics from Duke University.
All views expressed are my own, and do not reflect those of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York nor Federal Reserve System.
Interests. Macroeconomics, business cycles, monetary economics, macroeconometrics
